Doughnut

PREP TIME


15 Minutes

COOK TIME


15 Minutes

Print

Back

Ingredients:

2½ cups flour.

1½ teaspoon active dry yeast3 tablespoons of butter, cut into small pieces.

3 tablespoons of sugar.

¼ teaspoon salt.

½ cup warm milk.

1 large egg, slightly beaten.

2 cups vegetable oil, for frying .

½ cup of powdered sugar or white sugar

How to prepare:

01 Put the flour in a bowl with the yeast and stir to mix them together.

02 Add the butter and rub it with the flour, being careful not to leave any lumps.

03 Add sugar and salt, mix them, then make a hole in the middle, add eggs and milk in the middle of the hole, then mix using a flat plastic spoon until

04 the ingredients are completely mixed. Using our hands, we make a ball out of the mixture.

05 Sprinkle some flour on the surface of the work table and knead the dough on it for about 5 minutes until the dough becomes completely smooth.
